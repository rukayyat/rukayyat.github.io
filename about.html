About Us


Rukie's Touch was born out of need. It was born to make women from Africa and from around the whole world both old and young feel good about themselves. Beauty is in the eye of the beholder at Rukie's Touch. Everyone has an opportunity to explore their full potential.


Our CEO.
Rukayyat Awwal Gambo born May 30th in Lagos State Nigeria is a multitalented Make Up Artist And Chef. She is currently finishing her degree in Multi media and design at the American university of Nigeria (AUN). She was born with the will and drive to succeed. Rukie’s touch was born out of a passion by Rukayyat to make every woman on earth realise her true beauty. She’s a woman of class and elegance. She is also the ceo I’d include food company here and more. She loves cooking, 2pac, 2baba and more. She’s a wonderful person with a big heart that just wants every woman to see her real beauty. She always says “In every product of ours there’s love, quality and beauty”. She’s one of a kind in beauty and in persona.
